Our Place

Private, brand new vacation rental located in West Bay, approximately 200 yards from West Bay Beach, our home, Casa De Serenidad, provides fully furnished accommodations, featuring 3 bedrooms with queen-sized beds and full bathrooms on each floor as well as a purified water system throughout the house. This beautifully finished, property located in one of the best sites of the West Bay Area. Its position takes advantage of trade wind breezes and spectacular panoramic views from the rooftop deck. This property can sleep up to 6 people. We prefer children are over 10 yrs. No pets, please.


Location

The gorgeous West Bay Beach where you will find a variety of restaurants and entertainment less then 5 minute walking distance from the house. The best snorkling and scuba diving spots only a minute away from the house. Along with the beautiful beaches and Gumbalimba Park 10 minutes drive away this is the perfect spot for a varied and relaxing vacation.

Do Not Miss

Along with the beautiful beaches, you also should visit Gumbalimba Park located 10 min away from our house in West Bay, Roatan. This beachfront park offers not only the water activities and leisure of the beach and ocean, but also diverse attractions in over 20 lovely acres. There's a treasure to be found here for everyone in the family.
